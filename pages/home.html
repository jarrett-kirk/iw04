Welcome


Welcome to the LFSR Learning Hub, your go-to resource for exploring the fascinating world of Linear Feedback Shift Registers (LFSRs) in cryptography and cybersecurity. Our mission is to demystify this powerful cryptographic tool and provide an engaging learning experience. In the field of cryptography, visuals and friendly learning materials can be hard to come by. Our motivation is to bridge this gap and empower students and enthusiasts to grasp the concepts of LFSRs with ease.

Website Functions


Our website offers two key sections for your learning journey:


Visualization Page:


Dive into an interactive LFSR visualization that illustrates the inner workings of these registers. Explore the different configurations, toggle taps, and see how LFSRs generate sequences.


Coding Module Page:


Practice your skills by coding your own LFSR implementations. Our built-in code editor allows you to experiment with various LFSR parameters and see the results in real-time.
